Final Project Pitch Ideas

William Herkewitz

Pitch 1: Voting and Personal Climate Change — I'd like to collect data from both Wunderground or Weatherbase and do some excel magic to figure out the average temperatures (and extreme temperature variability) for the hometowns of each senator/congressman over the last 10 years. I'd also pull voting records from Data.gov. Comparing the hometown weather data to each of the senator/congressman's voting record, I'd ask the question: Does hometown temperature change effect how senators/congressmen vote on climate change?

Pitch 2: Baseball Nerves — Here I'd want to ask a simple question: Does the amount a baseball, basketball, or football player is talked and tweeted about effect how well they play their upcoming games? Does social media effect the nerves? I'm still thinking about where I'd pull social networking data, but Infochimps or ScraperWiki seem like good jumping off points. I would grab data for the players at here, here, or here. I'd compare the twitter mentions for each player with some metric in their upcoming game: errors made, fumbles, or shots missed. Something like that.
